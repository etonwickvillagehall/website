Events

Events

The Village Hall Committee sponsor and organise two community events on an annual basis, a Children’s Christmas Party in December and an Easter Egg Hunt, normally held on the Friday before Easter. In addition they also run an
annual Scarecrow Competition for all Eton Wick residents and businesses in conjunction with the Horticultural Show.

A number of groups and classes use the Village Hall on a regular basis and timings and contact details are to be found here.

The Village Hall also hosts a range of community events, and those open to the local community. It provides a meeting place for the Eton Wick Village Association.
